MY TOP 3 BEST TOURIST SPOTS

Batad Rice Terraces

The most well-known of these terraces are located in Ifugao province. 5 clusters of these have been declared UNESCO World Heritage Sites. All of them have been constructed by hand, using primitive tools. What makes these terraces unique and interesting is their continued use for 2,000 years which UNESCO says is an “enduring illustration of an ancient civilization that surpassed various challenges and setbacks posed by modernization.

Puerto Princessa Subterranean River

It’s dubbed nature’s greatest theme park but to get inside you need to brave its blindfold-dark, spookily quiet surrounds. It’s like something out of an Indiana Jones movie – an 8.2km underground river that bores through a limestone mountain cloaked in old-growth forest.


BORACAY

The beautiful island of Boracay has been named as one of the World’s Greatest Places of 2022, according to the prestigious yearly list of international magazine TIME.
